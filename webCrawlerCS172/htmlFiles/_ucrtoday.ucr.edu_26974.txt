      <h2>Doubling Down on American Idol</h2>

      <p class="p2">RIVERSIDE, Calif. (www.ucr.edu) — Viewers of “American Idol” on Fox Thursday, Jan. 29, saw not one, but two members of the UC Riverside community. And both made it through to the next round.</p> 
      <p>First, 18-year-old UC Riverside freshman Adanna Duru sang a cover of Lady Gaga’s “<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7CGIa4-m88" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y7CGIa4-m88']);">You and I</a>” to great praise from the judges.</p> 
      <p>A little later, Rocky Peter Ajoku, a 2013 UC Riverside graduate, sang his own composition: “<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DeHGX8cJBYI" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DeHGX8cJBYI']);">Wrong Places</a>,” and he was also given a golden ticket to allow him through to the next round.</p> 
      <p>Ajoku, who earned a degree in biological sciences in 2013, is featured on the show’s billboards around the country&nbsp;with&nbsp;the&nbsp;golden ticket in his hand.</p> 
      <div id="attachment_26676" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 270px">
       <a href="http://ucrtoday.ucr.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Rocky-Ajoku.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-26676" src="http://ucrtoday.ucr.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Rocky-Ajoku.jpg" alt="Rocky Peter Ajoku" width="270" height="172"></a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">Rocky Peter Ajoku is on American Idol this Season</p>
      </div> 
      <p>He has a dramatic story of hardship to tell, spending some of his childhood fending for himself and his brother in Nigeria. He was born in the U.S. but his parents split when he was just two and his mother took her children back to her homeland. He made his way back to the states and found himself in Fontana, but still did not have many resources. He worked hard at a community college and transferred into UCR, working in a lab during his time in school.</p> 
      <p class="p1">He remembers his time at UCR fondly. &nbsp;“UCR is more than a school.&nbsp;It is a family full of people who share a common goal of love and service&nbsp;to our community,” he said. With his&nbsp;community service group Legends, Ajoku volunteered by&nbsp;cleaning local streets around campus every weekend and offering free tutoring programs in local high schools every week day. “If I had to choose a university to attend all over again, UCR will always be my number one choice,” he added.</p> 
      <p>Ajoku created a lot of music on campus, in acoustically pleasing stairwells, at night by the bell tower, and even at a local donut shop. He also entertained at school events, such as freshman orientation.</p> 
      <p>Duru told a story about how her mother pushed her to sing from an early age.She was on “The Voice” at the age of 15 and she has tried out for other reality shows as well, including “America’s Got Talent.”</p> 
      <p>About 70 people gathered in East Lothian, a UCR residence hall, to watch the show Thursday night.</p> 
      <p>After her audition, Lady Gaga tweeted to her:</p> 
      <p>Just watched <a class="twitter-atreply pretty-link" dir="ltr" href="https://twitter.com/adannaduru" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://twitter.com/adannaduru']);"><s>@</s><b>adannaduru</b></a> sing “You and I” for her American Idol audition! <a class="twitter-timeline-link" dir="ltr" title="https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=DzrSr5QJHSg" href="https://t.co/SRpyCTRhOL" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://t.co/SRpyCTRhOL']);" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" data-expanded-url="https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=DzrSr5QJHSg"><span class="invisible">https://</span><span class="js-display-url">m.youtube.com/watch?v=DzrSr5</span><span class="invisible">QJHSg</span><span class="tco-ellipsis"><span class="invisible">&nbsp;</span>…</span></a> GO GIRL GET THAT AUDTION, work that song!</p>
